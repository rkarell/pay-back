PayBack

When using applications that help you share bills, I started to wonder the algorithms used to optimize the problem. I thought it would be a nice brain-teaser to figure it out and so I took my shot at the case.

Originally, I created a CLI app to just test the designed algorithms but ended up turning it into a web application for demonstration purposes. Here it is. Still lacks a proper UI, though.

Just fill in all the purchases you did. You will be provided an optimal way for paying back the debts.

Syntax: Buyer;Total amount of the purchase;Participants separated with commas (buyer automatically participates in the purchase)
